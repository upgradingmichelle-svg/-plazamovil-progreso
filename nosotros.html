Nuestra misionOur mission

Plaza Movil nacio para conectar a los negocios locales de Progreso, Yucatan con su comunidad — residentes, expats, snowbirds y visitantes. Queremos que encontrar un negocio local sea tan facil como un tap en tu telefono.Plaza Movil was born to connect local businesses in Progreso, Yucatan with their community — residents, expats, snowbirds and visitors. We want finding a local business to be as easy as a tap on your phone.

📱
Disenado para ProgresoDesigned for Progreso

Somos un directorio 100% local, bilingue (Espanol e Ingles), disenado para funcionar perfectamente en tu celular. Sin apps que descargar — solo abre el navegador y encuentra lo que necesitas.We are a 100% local, bilingual (Spanish and English) directory, designed to work perfectly on your phone. No apps to download — just open your browser and find what you need.
